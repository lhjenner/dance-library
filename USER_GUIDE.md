## Getting Started

### First Time Setup

1. **Sign In with Google**
   - Click "Sign in with Google" on the home screen
   - Authorize Dance Library to access your account

2. **Connect YouTube**
   - Click "Connect YouTube Account"
   - Grant permissions to manage your YouTube playlists
   - Your playlists will automatically sync

### Understanding the Interface

The app has two main views:
- **Playlists View**: See all your YouTube playlists
- **Video Player View**: Watch videos and mark segments

## Managing Playlists

### Syncing Playlists

- Click **"Sync Playlists"** to fetch the latest playlists from YouTube
- Videos are automatically sorted with newest first
- New videos added to YouTube appear after syncing

### Creating Playlists

1. Click **"Create"** button
2. Enter a playlist title
3. Press Enter or click **"Create"**
4. The playlist is created in both Dance Library and YouTube

### Organizing Playlists

- **Drag and drop** playlists to reorder them
- Your custom order is saved automatically
- Reordering here does NOT affect YouTube order

### Renaming Playlists

1. Click the **pencil icon** on a playlist
2. Type the new name
3. Click **"Save"** or press Enter
4. Changes sync to YouTube automatically

### Deleting Playlists

1. Click the **trash icon** on a playlist
2. Confirm the deletion
3. Playlist is deleted from both Dance Library and YouTube
4. All videos remain in your YouTube library

### Show/Hide Empty Playlists

- Toggle **"Show empty"** checkbox
- Empty playlists are hidden by default
- Your preference is saved across sessions

## Working with Videos

### Viewing Videos

1. Click on any playlist
2. See all videos with thumbnails and titles
3. Videos with no tags show an orange **"No tags"** badge

### Filtering Videos

**Filter by Tag:**
- Click any tag button to filter videos
- Multiple tags can be selected (OR logic)
- Shows count of filtered videos

**Untagged Only:**
- Click **"Untagged Only"** to see videos without tags
- Helps you identify videos that need tagging

**Clear Filters:**
- Click active tag buttons again to deselect
- All videos appear when no filters are active

### Video Operations

**Move Videos:**
1. Click **"Select"** to enter selection mode
2. Check videos to move
3. Click **"Move"**
4. Choose destination playlist
5. Confirm - videos move to new playlist

**Copy Videos:**
1. Select videos (same as Move)
2. Click **"Copy"**
3. Select one or more destination playlists
4. Videos are copied (originals remain)

**Remove Videos:**
- Click the **trash icon** on any video
- Confirm deletion
- Video removed from playlist (not from YouTube)

### Playing Videos

- Click any video card to open the player
- Video opens with your preferred playback speed
- Last watched video is remembered

## Marking Segments

Segments let you isolate specific parts of a video for practice.

### Click to Mark

1. **While video plays**, click **"Set Start"** when the move begins
2. Continue watching
3. Click **"Set End"** when the move ends
4. Segment is automatically saved

### Manual Time Entry

If you know exact timestamps:

1. Enter start time (format: **MM:SS**, e.g., `1:30`)
2. Enter end time (e.g., `2:15`)
3. Click **"Add"**
4. Segment is created

**Time Format Tips:**
- `0:30` = 30 seconds
- `1:45` = 1 minute 45 seconds
- `12:00` = 12 minutes

### Playing Segments

- Each segment shows start/end times
- Click **"Play"** button on any segment
- Video jumps to start and stops at end automatically

### Segment Tags & Notes

**Add Tags:**
1. Type tag name in segment's tag input
2. Press Enter
3. Tag appears as blue badge

**Add Notes:**
1. Type notes in segment's text field
2. Notes save automatically when you click away

**Remove Tags:**
- Click the **√ó** on any tag to remove it

**Delete Segment:**
- Click **trash icon** on segment
- Segment is permanently deleted

## Using Tags

Tags help organize and find your dance moves.

### Video-Level Tags

Apply to the entire video:
- Type tag in **"Video Tags"** field
- Press Enter to add
- Example tags: `"whip"`, `"lindy hop"`, `"advanced"`

### Segment-Level Tags

Apply to specific segments:
- More specific than video tags
- Example: `"reverse whip"`, `"open position"`
- Video inherits both video and segment tags

### Tag Best Practices

**Use consistent names:**
- ‚úÖ `"whip"` everywhere
- ‚ùå `"whip"`, `"whips"`, `"Whip"` mixed

**Be specific with segments:**
- Video: `"whip"`, `"lindy hop"`
- Segment 1: `"reverse whip"`
- Segment 2: `"open whip"`

**Use multiple tags:**
- Style: `"lindy hop"`, `"west coast swing"`
- Level: `"beginner"`, `"intermediate"`, `"advanced"`
- Move type: `"whip"`, `"swing out"`, `"sugar push"`

## Playback Controls

### Speed Control

- Click speed buttons: **0.25x, 0.5x, 0.75x, 1x, 1.25x, 1.5x, 2x**
- Your preferred speed is saved
- New videos open at your last used speed
- Useful for learning: slow down to 0.5x or 0.75x

### Notes

- Add notes in the **"Notes"** section
- Notes save automatically when you click away
- Use for:
  - Key teaching points
  - Things to remember
  - Personal reminders

## Mobile Features

### Portrait Mode

- Responsive layout optimized for phones
- Larger buttons for easy tapping
- Video titles show 3 lines (vs 2 on desktop)
- Buttons stack vertically

### Landscape Mode

Rotate your phone for an enhanced viewing experience:

**Almost-Fullscreen Video:**
- Video fills most of the screen
- Controls bar appears below video

**Quick Controls:**
- **Left side:** Speed controls (0.25x, 0.5x, 0.75x, 1x)
- **Right side:** Set Start, Set End buttons
- **Back button:** Return to playlist

- Touch-manipulation class removes 300ms tap delay

## Tips & Tricks

### Efficient Tagging Workflow

1. **First pass:** Add video-level tags (style, level)
2. **Watch through:** Mark all segments
3. **Second pass:** Add specific segment tags
4. Use **"Untagged Only"** filter to find untagged videos

### Practice Sessions

1. Filter by specific tag (e.g., `"whip"`)
2. Work through all videos/segments with that tag
3. Use slow speed (0.5x-0.75x) for learning
4. Normal speed (1x) for practice
5. Add notes about progress

### Organization Strategy

**Create playlists by:**
- Dance style: "Lindy Hop Basics", "WCS Advanced"
- Event: "Camp Jitterbug 2025", "Workshops"
- Goal: "Moves to Learn", "Performance Routine"

**Use tags for:**
- Cross-playlist searching
- Skill level tracking
- Move categories

### Keyboard Shortcuts

**Video Player:**
- `Enter` - Add tag (when typing in tag field)
- `Escape` - Cancel tag input
- `Tab` - Move between form fields

**Modals:**
- `Enter` - Confirm action
- `Escape` - Cancel/close

### Speed Selection

**0.25x-0.5x:** Super slow - analyzing technique
**0.75x:** Slow practice - learning new moves  
**1x:** Normal - actual practice
**1.25x-1.5x:** Fast - challenge yourself
**2x:** Preview - quick review

### Cross-Device Sync

- Settings sync across devices (desktop, phone, tablet)
- Playlists, videos, tags, and segments available everywhere
- Your preferred speed and last playlist saved
- Sign in on multiple devices to access your library

### Performance Tips

- Large playlists (90+ videos) load in under 2 seconds
- Segments are cached for instant playback
- Network required for first load, then works offline
- Videos stream from YouTube (internet required)

## Troubleshooting

**Playlists not showing:**
- Click "Sync Playlists"
- Check YouTube connection
- Refresh the page

**Video won't play:**
- Check internet connection
- Video may be deleted from YouTube
- Try reloading the page

**Tags not saving:**
- Check internet connection
- Try again in a few seconds
- Changes may take a moment to sync

**Slow loading:**
- First load after sync takes longer
- Subsequent loads are much faster
- Check your internet speed

- Sign out from any device to end session
